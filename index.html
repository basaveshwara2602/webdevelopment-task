Know about corona virus (covid 19)!!!!

1.) Corona is a virus family that consists a total of seven virus. Among which four virus are not harmful to humans other 3 virus are harmful to human . The three harmful virus are named as follows ( SARS, MERS & COVID19). The SARS & MERS virus have also affected people but the death ratio is very low when compare to covid19.
2.) Covid 19 started spreading from an animal called pangolin.
3.) The symptoms of covid 19 are, Initially the affected person will have normal cold and fever then it slowly affects the lungs causing respiratory problems eventually leading to death.
4.) Covid 19 has the capacity to affect the all age groups but the people above aged, sugar patients and BP patients are at high risk of being affected sooner then a normal person.
